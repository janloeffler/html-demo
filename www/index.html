Jan Löffler is CTO of Plesk and is responsible for driving technical innovation, developing Plesk roadmaps and leading all aspects of the company‘s technology strategy and vision.

Before joining Plesk he was Head of Platform Engineering at Zalando and responsible for overseeing the company’s system engineering, identity & access management, continuous delivery, developer tooling teams as well as driving Zalando’s Open Source activities.

In the early days of his career Jan was Head of Development Webhosting & Server at 1&1 Internet AG and became a true expert and trusted advisor in the hosting industry.

Member of the World Hosting Days Content Advisory Board
Co-organizer of the WordPress community in Karlsruhe
